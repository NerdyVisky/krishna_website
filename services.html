Krishna Services

Since the past 27 years, Krishna Hospital has been consistently offering the best-in-class gynec and obstretic services, from successfully conducting hundreds of high-risk medical procedures to providing excellent hospitality to its patients.

Medical Procedures

Every doctor at Krishna has decades of working experience in various high-risk situations behind them. Through these 27 years, Dr Nilesh Trivedi, himself has been involved in hundreds of critical situations. And through his skills and experience combined, he has exhibited a staggering success rate at these procedures.

Moreover, Krishna Hospital is a destination where technology meets excellence. Along with a highly experienced working team, Krishna Hospital has incorporated an array of cutting-edge technological equipments into its services. However, the quality that differentiates Krishna from other hospitals is the extremely affordable pricing of such high-standard services. Illustrated below are the usual medical services and procedures offered at Krishna.

Routine Consultation

Book an OPD appointment with Dr. Nilesh Trivedi for any sort of gynec/obstretic medical issue/advice.

High-Risk Pregnancy Care

Provides assistance and care for any kind of high-risk pregnancy situations.

Infertility Treatments

Experience parenthood through Krishna's efficient infertility treatments.

3D and 4D Sonography

High-resolution Voluson sonography machines equipped with 3D/4D capabilities and colour doppler.

Key-Hole Surgery (Laproscopy)

Vaginal Hystrectomy, all-kinds-of Open Gynec Surgeries and Vaginal Surgeries are available.

Krishna has an in-house baby vaccination facility to keep your new-born free from any potential ailments.

Room Categories

Over the years, Krishna Hospital has been renowned for its excellent facilites and hospitality. Krishna Hospital has a total of 15 hospital beds present in 7 rooms and one general ward facility. The rooms are divided in three categories so that the patients have the freedom to pick their room of choice.

Each room consists of one patient bed which is equipped with blood-pressure, oxygen saturation and pulse-rate monitors. Moreover, each room also contains accommodation facilites for the patient's relatives. Depending on the room category, several rooms also offer room air-conditioning, television, fridge, inter-com speakers and other amenities.

Deluxe Rooms

Deluxe rooms are the most premier rooms offered at Krishna. A total of 4 rooms, with each room offering complete air-conditioning, cable television, seperate fridge, etc. Moreover, food services like breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks and drinks for both the patient and its relatives is available without any extra charges.

Special Rooms

Special Rooms are the second class of rooms at Krishna. Apart from the usual medical facilities for the patients, these rooms offer cable television, seperate fridge, relatives accommodation and inter-com speakers. However, these rooms are non-air conditioned and provide food services only on seperate demand.

Semi-Special Rooms

Semi-Special Rooms are the third class of rooms at Krishna. These non-air conditioned rooms offer extremely affordable accommodation with limited facilites. Patient relatives accommodation is available within the room. Other facilities might be made available on additional charges.

General Ward

These are the fourth class of patient accommodation facility at Krishna. It consists of a large common medical facility which houses 5 beds equipped with medical equipments. Being the most cost-friendly facility, most of the hospitality services can be made available on additional charges.

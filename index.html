About Krishna

Established in 1994 by Dr. Nilesh Trivedi, Krishna Hospital has been committed to a holistic philosophy of motherhood and women's care.

Since the last 27 years, Krishna hospital has been consistently providing women care services ranging from regular consulation and 3D/4D Sonography to gynec operative procedures like Cesarean section, Hystrectomy, Laproscopy, Colposcopy and many more.

Through its well-trained 22 member staff team and state-of-the-art medical equipments, every patient at Krishna Hospital is assured of a flawless and comfortable medicare experience.
